Women in Film and Literature

Over the years, the gender ratio of authors and film directors have made generous strides in balancing the gap. Our society has renovated itself slowly, giving women rights to their bodies, their careers and their aspirations. Women are no longer shunned behind the titles of "mommy" or "housewife". Now, they are welcome, through the feminist movement, to education and creative freedom.

Although females are still extremely underrepresented in the film industry, they have made a significant stride in 2020 with a 5% increase since 2019. This is the highest recorded increase in female representation ever recorded.

As time has progressed, women have made their way onto the best-seller lists. Between the 50's-90's women had half the representation male writers had, outnumbering women 3 to 1. Starting in the 2000's the gap decreased and those numbers have come closer to 1 to 1.
